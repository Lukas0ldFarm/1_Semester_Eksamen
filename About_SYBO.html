About SYBO

SYBO is a Danish game studio founded in 2010 by Bodie Jahn-Mulliner and Sylvester Rishøj Jensen. Their passion for animation and creativity inspired the creation of Subway Surfers. Together with Kiloo Games, they developed the worldwide hit game Subway Surfers.
