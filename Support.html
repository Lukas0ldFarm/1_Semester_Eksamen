Support

Find answers to common Subway Surfer questions

How do I update Subway Surfers?

To update Subway Surfers, go to the App Store or Google Play, search for the game and tap Update.
If you don’t see the button, the game is already up to date.

Why is my game lagging?

– Close apps running in the background
– Restart your device
– Make sure your storage isn’t full
– Update the game to the newest version

Where can I find World Tour info?

World Tour information can be found inside the game under the Events tab. Each update features a new city, a themed character, and exclusive challenges.

My progress didn’t save — what do I do?

Make sure your game is connected to either:
– Facebook
– Google Play Games
– Apple Game Center
